About Me

I am an Early Career Principal Investigator (PI) at the Centre for Frontier AI Research (CFAR), A*STAR, working with Prof. Ivor Tsang. My research bridges sequential decision-making and language model reasoning to develop scalable, verifiable, and self-evolving agents capable of long-horizon reasoning, autonomous adaptation, and reliable execution in the open world.

Before joining A*STAR, I was a Research Scientist at Sea AI Lab (SAIL) and previously worked at Baidu Research USA in Seattle. I obtained both my Ph.D. and B.Eng. degrees from the College of Computing and Data Science (CCDS), Nanyang Technological University (NTU) where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Sinno Jialin Pan.
